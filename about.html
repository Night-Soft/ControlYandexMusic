Yandex Music Control!

App to control Yandex music from any tabs, windows or apps!
The app allows to:
  • Togle a track.
  • Add or remove track from your favorites.
  • Use keyboard keys.
  • View and open a cover of any track.
  • You can use buttons of this extension or your keyboard.
  • When you use your keyboard to control, you can see a track's name in notifictions.
Default keyboard shortcuts:
  • Next track (Ctrl+Shift+9)
  • Pause track (Ctrl+Shift+8)
  • Previous track (Ctrl+Shift+7)
To change it, go to Chrome>Extensions>☰>Keyboard Shortcuts.
After you changed keyboard shortcuts, you may need to restart your browser.

To start using the extension, open tab with Yandex music and enable your track. Do not close the tab! Done!
